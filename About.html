OUR STORY

COFFEE. ROASTED. PROPERLY.

THE COFFEE YOU DESERVE.

Our mission at The Truth Coffee Roasting is to create extraordinary coffee experiences. Their mission is to make the best possible coffee for South African consumers, offering a variety of bold and full-flavored blends. The roasting process is meticulous, with a focus on ethical sourcing and innovative coffee solutions for both individual and business needs. Truth Café has been recognized as one of the best coffee shops globally, showcasing their commitment to exceptional coffee quality and customer experience.

At Truth Coffee Roasting, our vision is to be the heartbeat of coffee culture in Cape Town — a bold, immersive space where passion, craftsmanship, and community meet. We envision a coffee shop that goes beyond serving exceptional coffee. Truth Coffee Roasting will stand as a symbol of authenticity, creativity, and excellence — where every cup tells a story of ethically sourced beans, masterful roasting, and uncompromising quality.

Co-founded by Andrzej Janik and David Donde, pioneers of speciality coffee in Cape Town, our mission was to create extraordinary coffee experiences, and we did. Truth Café has been recognised as the best in the world several times over by publications both abroad and in our very own backyard.

If you're looking for bold, full-flavoured coffees, look no further than Truth.

At Truth, we've mastered inimitable blends, crafted to bring you world-class coffee, every time. Whether you're more into dark and intense, or smooth and balanced, look no further. We have the perfect brew for you.
