WELCOME TO THE AFRICAN SCHOOL OF COFFEE (ASC)

We believe in the transformative power of coffee. It's not just about mastering the craft; it's about fostering connections, sparking new opportunities, and building something great together.

Nestled in the heart of Cape Town, ASC is a powerful collaboration brought together by industry pioneers; a premier non-profit company with a mission to elevate the African coffee industry through education, empowerment, and a deep commitment to quality.

From cultivation and roasting to brewing and tasting, our curriculum is designed to empower coffee enthusiasts, baristas, and entrepreneurs alike. Here, students will gain hands-on experience through practical training with expert instructors in Africa's premier coffee training facility.

Join us and be part of a global movement that celebrates the artistry and exploration of coffee in Africa.
